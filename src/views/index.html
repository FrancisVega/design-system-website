Carbon
Design System

Thoughtful, detailed guidelines that enable teams to confidently design delightful experiences.

Get the Design Kit Get the Developer Kit

Carbon in nature allows for complex structures to be made from simpler compounds. The Carbon Design System is a series of individual styles and components, that when combined make beautifully complex, natural, and intuitive designs. These designs are automatically systemic and logical, as they all follow the same universal principles. Carbon is constantly evolving and growing as we learn from our users, and most importantly, from you.

Elements

Consider elements as the deepest level of the Design System. These are very specific UI elements and patterns that make up a larger experience, such as buttons, links, and checkboxes.

Components

Certain elements are combined and reused based on the experience or the context. Components are composed of one or more elements, such as tables, cards, and activity logs.

Templates

Templates provide a base structure for specific overviews built within Bluemix. Use these as a starting point and foundation for your design to be built around.

At IBM Design, we believe the experiences we design should communicate the same brand promise. Carbon uses the IBM Design Language as our guiding principle. These core values remind us to design for an outcome and be authentically thoughtful.
