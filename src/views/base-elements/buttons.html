Primary Buttons

Primary buttons should be used for the principal call to action on the page. What is the most important action your user should take? Primary buttons should be three words or less.

Sets of Buttons

Sets of buttons are used when action required by the user may have more than one option. Always use a positive action button paired with a negative action button. Positive action buttons should be presented on the right. Negative action buttons will be on the left.
