Detail pages are used for showing information and details of an instance that exists in the user's dashboard. They appear when a user clicks on a specific item in their dashboard. Typically, these instances can include CF Apps, Services, Virtual Machines, and Containers. Use these templates as a starting point and foundation for your design.

Service Details

Service consoles may be accessed from the dashboard after a user provisions a service from the Bluemix catalog. Service consoles serve as locations for users to learn more about services, configure their service instance and connect it to an existing app or service instance.

Learn

Provide pertinent introductory material such as links to service-specific API documentation and demos so users quickly and effectively get up and running.

Configure

Enable users to easily integrate a service into their workflow. Configuring a service should be accessible.
