Service Tags

Bluemix uses tags to categorize services in the catalog.

  • IBM: is an IBM branded service.
  • Beta: The service is in the final stages of development and needs some final polish and refinement.
  • Community: A community-driven service that is open sourced.
  • Third-Party: The service is from a Third Party vendor.
