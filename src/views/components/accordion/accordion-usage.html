{{#markdown}} ## General Accordions are best used when users need to consume specific areas of content within a page or when vertical space is limited. Use accordions when there is enough content to condense. Avoid nested accordions because it increases the cognitive load for users. ## Interaction * Users may have multiple accordions open at the same time. * By default, one accordion should be open to inform the user there is collapsible content. * The entire header area is clickable to expand or collapse the content below. {{/markdown}}
