{{#markdown}} ## Format ### Size Generic notifications have a fixed width to maintain consistency, but their height can vary depending on content. Inline notifications width and height may vary depending on the type of content presented. ### Title All notifications should have titles to give users a quick means of understanding the content of the notification. ### Message The body or message of the notification must be contained within two lines. Be as descriptive as possible, and try to include troubleshooting actions or next steps. If you can communicate the main message of your notification to the user within the title, a secondary message may not be needed. ### Dismissal All notifications must have at least one method of dismissal. Typically, it is a small “x” in the upper right hand corner. ### Color

Green 30: Success

Yellow 30: Warning

Red 40: Error

Blue 20: Informational

### Icons Icons can provide additional clarity around the purpose of a notification. They should be placed to the left of the title. See the Style tab for further specs about icons.
## Placement ### Generic Notifications These should always appear in the top right corner, and slide in from off canvas. Actionable notifications do not appear on mobile screen widths.
