{{#markdown}} ## Format ### Size Generic notifications have a fixed width but the height may vary depending on the content. The width and height of inline notifications may vary depending on content. ### Title All notifications have subject titles. ### Message The body of the notification should be contained within two lines. Be descriptive and include any troubleshooting actions or next steps. When possible, communicate the main message using the just the title. ### Dismissal All notifications have at least one method of dismissal. Typically, it is a small “x” in the upper right hand corner. ### Color

Green 30: Success

Yellow 30: Warning

Red 40: Error

Blue 20: Informational

### Icons Icons may provide additional clarity. Icons should be placed to the left of a title. See the Style tab for further specs about icons.
## Placement ### Generic notifications Generic notifications slide in and out a page from the top-right corner. Actionable notifications do not appear on mobile screen widths.
