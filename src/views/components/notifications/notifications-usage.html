{{#markdown}} ## Format ### Size To maintain consistency, generic notifications have a fixed width but the height may vary depending on content. The width and height of inline notifications may vary depending on content. ### Title All notifications have titles that inform the subject of the content presented. ### Message The body or message of the notification must be contained within two lines. Be as descriptive as possible, and try to include troubleshooting actions or next steps. If you can communicate the main message of your notification to the user within the title, a secondary message may not be needed. ### Dismissal All notifications must have at least one method of dismissal. Typically, it is a small “x” in the upper right hand corner. ### Color

Green 30: Success

Yellow 30: Warning

Red 40: Error

Blue 20: Informational

### Icons Icons may provide additional clarity. They should be placed to the left of the title. See the Style tab for further specs about icons.
## Placement ### Generic Notifications Generic notifications always slide in and out a page from the top-right corner. Actionable notifications do not appear on mobile screen widths.
