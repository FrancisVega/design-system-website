Foundation

The Content Switcher is used for large groups of content, as opposed to the Toggle, which is meant for a "yes"/"no" or "on"/"off" binary decision. An example situation for a Content Switcher is in the case of a form in which some of the fields are dependent on the selected option of a prior field.

Other Considerations

  • There should be a default selection based on usage. This would be the first option.
  • No more than two words may be used for any title.
  • It is recommended to keep Content Switchers to binary selection. However, three to five options may be used given the circumstances. Discretion should be exercised when using more than two options.
