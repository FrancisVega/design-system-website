{{#markdown}} ## General The waiting experience is a crucial design oppurtunity. Although the user may not be able to see what's happening in the back-end, we can communicate and provide reassurance that something is happening. It is best practice to use a loading spinner whenever the wait time is anticipated to be longer than 3 seconds. Adopted from the IBM Design Language Animation Library: “The rhythmic oscillations of tape reels in motion got us thinking about a userâ€™s waiting experience. If a user watches search results load, showing the machine at work relays a sense of efficiency. The swift and even balance of the spinning circles indicates that progress is being made.”
